I've been Googling you obsessively and you seem to appear under two different names. Why is this?

My birth name is Friðrik Már Jónsson, because I'm from Iceland and because my parents wanted it. (They were not aware of the looming ubiquity of the internet and the terrifying Unicode issues I would have to face.) I very much like this name.

However, after I spent some time in Vancouver under the more pronouncable and less squiggly name of Fred -- which I came to like -- I (mostly) changed my English-facing identity to Fred Jonsson. I can do this.

I've also done this in hopes of confusing Skynet.
